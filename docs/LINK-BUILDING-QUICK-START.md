## ‚ùì FAQ: Getting Started

**Q: I don't have time for 17 hours in Week 1. What's the minimum?**
A: Focus on Google Business Profile (3h) + Essential Citations (5h) = 8 hours. Everything else can wait.

**Q: What if journalists don't respond?**
A: Normal! Expect 20-30% response rate. Follow up after 1 week, then move on.

**Q: Should I pay for directory listings?**
A: No. All directories in this plan have free options. Paid "featured" listings are optional.

**Q: Can I automate social media posting?**
A: Yes, use Buffer (free plan: 3 accounts, 10 posts each). But engagement must be manual.

**Q: What if Wikipedia editors reject my edits?**
A: Post suggestions on Talk page instead of direct edits. Let neutral editors implement.
